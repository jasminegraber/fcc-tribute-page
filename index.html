Tao Porchon-Lynch

World's Oldest Yoga Teacher

A Yogi and Competitive Dancer

  • 1918: Born in Puducherry, India on August 13
  • 1922: Became interested in yoga when she witnessed a group of youthful yoga practitioners on a beach
  • 1962: Married Bill Lynch but became widowed after her husband died in 1982
  • 1967: Assisted in the establishment of the American Wine Society (AWS) with her spouse
  • 1970: Elected to be the Vice-President of the AWS in Southern New York
  • 1976: Became one of the founders of the Yoga Teachers Alliance, now known as the Yoga Teachers Association
  • 1995: Flex to Israel to attend the Yoga for Peace International Peace Conferenc
  • 2012: Recognized by Guinness World Records as the world’s oldest yoga instructor at age 93
  • 2015: Appeared on Season 10 of America’s Got Talent
  • 2015: Called the “Real-Life Forrest Gump” in an ABC World News interview
  • 2015: Published her autobiography Dancing Light: The Spiritual Side of Being Through the Eyes of a Modern Yoga Master
  • 2016: Received the Women’s Entrepreneurship Day Pioneer Award at the United Nations in recognition of her achievements in the sports world
  • 2020: Died at the age of 101 on February 21
